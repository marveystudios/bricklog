Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: Bricklog is designed with your privacy in mind. Your collection data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information.

Introduction

Bricklog ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Marvey Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data Bricklog accesses, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Bricklog is designed to work entirely on your device. The app does not:

  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share any data with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

Bricklog stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your LEGO set collection data
  • Minifigure information
  • Wishlist items
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored using your device's local storage and iCloud (if enabled). We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

Bricklog may connect to the following third-party services:

  • Rebrickable API: Used to fetch LEGO set information, images, and data. When you search for sets, queries are sent to Rebrickable's servers. Please refer to Rebrickable's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.
  • Apple iCloud: If you have iCloud enabled, your collection data may sync across your devices via Apple's iCloud service. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account, security is managed by Apple's iOS security features and iCloud encryption.

Children's Privacy

Bricklog does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app can be used by users of all ages as it does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. You can delete all app data by uninstalling Bricklog from your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@marvey.app
